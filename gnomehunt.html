Gnome Hunt (2024)

A multi-platform party game.

Gnome Hunt is a multi-platform game that utilizes both a VR headset and mobile phones. The VR player is the exterminator, tasked with ridding a garden of imposter gnomes. The mobile phone players are the imposter gnomes, and are tasked with hiding from the exterminator. Gnome Hunt is inspired by games such as Prop Hunt, where players utilize their surroundings to avoid detection by the exterminator. The goal of the game depends on the role you play - the exterminator wants to rid the garden of all imposter gnomes, wheras the imposter gnomes want to survive the night. Gnome Hunt was displayed at three major events: ForskarFredag at AlbaNova, a showcase at Tekniska Muséet, as well as KulturNatten at Tekniska Muséet.

My work on Gnome Hunt

Character Design

My main responsibility for Gnome Hunt was all 3D art and assets. The first order of business within this responsibility was creating the characters utilized in the game. Using Blender, I made both a gnome character and a gremlin character. The initial idea was that the gnomes would reveal their true self (being gremlins), but unfortuntaly due to time constraints the gremlins did not make it into the final game. To create the characters, I began by utilizing blenders scultping tools to create high-poly versions. Once the high-poly models were created, I employed the technique of retopology to create low-poly versions of the models, baking the details of the high-poly models into normal maps.

Animation

Once the low-poly characters were complete, it was time to rig and animate them. I created the skeletons for each models by hand, and then utilized Blender's key points to animate the run cycles.

Props

To populate the garden of the game, I created a multitude of different 3D props. This included trees, hedges, grass, mushrooms, a house and the zap gun the VR player uses to exterminate the imposter gnomes.

Shaders

To bring more life into the vegetation of the game, I created two diffrent URP shaders within Unity. These shaders were created to bring some movement to the leaves and the grass, allowing them to sway in the wind.

Level Design

The last part of my work on Gnome Hunt related to putting together the garden in which the game takes place. This involved utilizing all the 3D assets I created, as well as some sourced textures and assets to create the garden.
